#97 TIFF 2022: Critics' Roundtable

Posted on Wednesday, Dec 7, 2022
Episode 97: Aseem Chhabra, Namrata Joshi and Aditya Shrikrishna join Baisakhi and Aparita for a TIFF 2022 round-up.

Show Notes

Episode 97: They returned - IRL! Aseem Chhabra, film journalist/festival director of NYIFF, and film critic/writer Namrata Joshi attended TIFF 2022. They decided to grace us with their presence at the critics’ roundtable, where they were joined by writer, critic and podcaster Aditya Shrikrishna. We missed Shubhra Gupta and Suparna Sharma while talking about films we saw at TIFF 2022.
